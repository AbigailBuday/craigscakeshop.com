Schedule Your Wedding Cake Tasting

We’ll create the perfect cake for your special day

At Craig’s Cake Shop, we couldn’t be more honored to be part of your special day. We promise to create the one-of-a-kind wedding cake of your dreams — from start to finish, we’ll work with you to make sure every detail is perfect.

Our process starts with a personalized wedding cake tasting at our Verona, Wisconsin location. In our private tasting room, we’ll spend 45 minutes to an hour sampling cake flavors and filling options, learning about your vision for your special wedding day, and dreaming up your perfect design.

To make sure we can give each couple the personalized attention they deserve, we schedule wedding tastings by appointment only on Wednesdays, Thursdays at 5 pm, and Saturdays.
